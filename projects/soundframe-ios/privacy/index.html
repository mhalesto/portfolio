Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

SoundFrame is a video editor for beat-synced edits, captions, layered clips, and exports on iPhone and iPad. Core media work is designed to stay local to your device. We aim to minimize the amount of personal data that leaves your device during normal editing use. This policy explains what information may be involved when you import media, generate captions, purchase subscriptions, and export finished videos.

Information We Collect

SoundFrame does not create advertising profiles, run third-party ad tracking, or sell personal information from your editing activity. We do not collect your project media for marketing purposes, and we do not build behavioral profiles based on your edits.

If Apple provides subscription transaction status or entitlement information, that data is used only to unlock and manage paid features inside the app. Basic device-level permission responses may also affect which features are available, but they are not used for advertising.

How Media Is Processed

Videos, photos, audio, captions, and timeline edits are primarily processed on your device. Imported media stays under your control unless you choose to export or share it.

When you use editing tools, the app works with files stored on your device or in your app container rather than uploading your library by default. Exported files are created only when you choose to render or save them, and shared files are sent only to the destination you explicitly pick.

Permissions

  • Photos and Media Library: to import media and save exports.
  • Speech Recognition: to generate timed captions from audio you choose.

Permissions are requested only when you use a feature that needs them, and you can decline a permission if you do not want to use that related workflow.

If you later change your mind, you can manage those permissions in iOS Settings. Denying a permission may prevent specific features from working, but it does not stop you from using the rest of the app.

Subscriptions and Payments

Optional subscriptions are handled by Apple. SoundFrame does not receive your full payment card details. Apple may provide transaction status needed to unlock subscription features.

Subscription renewals, cancellations, refunds, billing issues, and payment methods are managed through your App Store account under Apple's systems and policies. SoundFrame uses entitlement information returned by Apple only to determine whether Pro features should be available on your device.

Third-Party Services

The current release does not include third-party ad tracking. If caption model downloads or external services are added in the future, this policy will be updated before release.

If a future feature depends on a third-party service, we will describe that dependency before it is used in production.

Data Retention

SoundFrame does not store your project media on its own servers. Your edits and exported files stay on your device unless you choose to share them elsewhere.

Temporary files, caches, and generated assets may be created on device to support editing, previews, imports, captions, or exports. You can remove local files from within the app or by deleting the app from your device. Once you share or export media to another service or app, that destination handles the file under its own privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

SoundFrame is not directed to children under 13, and it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app or related support contact, please reach out so we can review and address the situation. Parents or guardians who have privacy concerns may contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Policy

If privacy practices change, this page will be updated before those changes take effect.

The date at the top of this page reflects the latest published revision of this policy.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact currenttech.co.za@gmail.com.

If you are contacting us about subscriptions, include the device and App Store account context needed to describe the issue, but do not send payment card details. If you are contacting us about privacy or data handling, include enough detail for us to identify the relevant feature or workflow.

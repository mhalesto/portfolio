SoundFrame iOS

Beat-synced video editing on iPhone and iPad.

SoundFrame helps creators build short-form edits with captions, layered media, audio control, filters, speed tools, and export-ready vertical clips.

Platform: iOS
Processing: Core editing and caption workflows run on device.
Account: No login is required for editing. An optional subscription unlocks Pro features.
Permissions: Photos access for import/export and Speech Recognition for timed captions.
  • Create edits with beat-aware cuts, trim tools, and timeline snapping.
  • Generate timed captions and refine text directly on the timeline.
  • Stack video, audio, graphics, text, and speed layers in one project.
  • Export polished clips for social-first formats.
